01.Overview

DOLPHLINK is operated by Baiwu Technology Group ("Baiwu", "we", "us", "our") from Republic Plaza, Singapore. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal data when you visit our website, use our communications platform, or engage our services.

This policy applies to all DOLPHLINK products, including the Communications Platform-as-a-Service (CPaaS), AI orchestration tools, the digital business card platform, and any related websites or applications operated by Baiwu. By accessing or using these services, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy TBD by counsel.

Plain summary

We collect what we need to deliver communications reliably and govern them defensibly. We do not sell personal data. We give you tools to access, correct, and delete your data, and we tell you when policies change.

02.Definitions

Capitalised terms used in this policy have the meanings set out below.

Personal Data
Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined under the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and equivalent regional laws.
Service
Any DOLPHLINK product, including websites, APIs, dashboards, AI orchestration, the messaging hub, and digital business cards.
Customer
An organisation or individual that contracts with Baiwu for use of the Service.
End User
An individual who interacts with a Customer through the Service (for example, a recipient of an SMS sent via DOLPHLINK).
Sub-processor
A third party engaged by Baiwu to process Personal Data on behalf of a Customer.

03.Information we collect

We collect information from three sources: directly from you, automatically as you use the Service, and from authorised third parties.

3.1 Information you provide

  • Account and contact details (name, organisation, role, business email, phone)
  • Authentication credentials and API keys
  • Billing and tax information
  • Content you submit through forms, support requests, or the Service itself
  • Communications metadata required to deliver messages (sender, recipient, channel, timestamps)

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • Log data (IP address, user agent, device identifiers, access times, referring page)
  • Service usage events (API calls, dashboard interactions, feature engagement)
  • Performance and reliability telemetry (latency, route health, delivery status)
  • Security signals (authentication attempts, anomaly detection events)

3.3 Information from third parties

  • Data from carrier partners and integration vendors necessary to deliver communications
  • Compliance and identity-verification information from authorised KYC providers
  • Information shared by you with our integration partners and provided to us with consent

04.How we use information

We use Personal Data only for purposes that are necessary or compatible with delivering and improving the Service. These include:

  • Service delivery: routing communications, authenticating users, processing payments, and providing dashboards and analytics
  • Reliability and security: monitoring performance, preventing abuse and fraud, investigating incidents, enforcing terms
  • Customer support: responding to enquiries, troubleshooting, training support agents on real cases (with anonymisation where practical)
  • Compliance: meeting regulatory and legal obligations including PDPA, GDPR, sectoral rules (MAS TRM, IM8) and lawful requests
  • Product development: evaluating feature usage, building new capabilities, conducting aggregated research
  • Communications: sending service-related notices, security alerts, and (with consent or legitimate interest) product updates

We do not use Personal Data for automated decisions that produce legal effects without human review.

05.Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell Personal Data. We may share Personal Data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Sub-processors engaged under written agreements with appropriate safeguards. A current list of Sub-processors is available on request.
  • Carrier and integration partners where their participation is necessary to deliver the Service (e.g. Tier-1 mobile operators routing SMS, regulated payment processors).
  • Professional advisers bound by confidentiality (auditors, accountants, legal counsel).
  • Legal and regulatory authorities where required by law, court order, or regulatory request, after applying appropriate scrutiny.
  • Successors in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganisation, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality safeguards.
  • With your direction where you instruct us to share data with a specified party.
No data sale

Baiwu does not sell, rent, or barter Personal Data, and does not share Personal Data with advertising networks for behavioural advertising.

06.International transfers

DOLPHLINK is headquartered in Singapore. Personal Data may be transferred internationally to deliver the Service. Where transfers are subject to data-protection laws, we apply appropriate safeguards including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (or equivalent) for transfers from the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland
  • ASEAN Cross-Border Data Flow mechanisms where applicable
  • Customer-elected regional data residency options for in-scope products
  • Encryption and access controls during transit and storage

07.Data retention

We retain Personal Data for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Specific retention periods depend on the data type, purpose, and applicable regulations.

Where Personal Data is no longer required, we either delete it or anonymise it so that it can no longer be associated with an individual.

Customers may configure shorter retention windows for in-scope data through their account settings or by written request. TBD by counsel — default retention schedule will be appended.

08.Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may exercise the following rights with respect to your Personal Data:

  • Access a copy of the Personal Data we hold about you
  • Rectification of inaccurate or incomplete Personal Data
  • Erasure of your Personal Data, subject to legal exceptions
  • Restriction of certain processing activities
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Singapore: the Personal Data Protection Commission)

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 13. We will verify your identity before responding and will reply within the timeframes required by applicable law.

09.Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect Personal Data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Our programme includes:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Identity-first access controls with role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication
  • Tamper-evident audit logging for sensitive operations
  • Continuous vulnerability management and quarterly third-party penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures aligned with industry standards
  • Personnel training and confidentiality obligations
  • ISO 27001 information-security management system

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security. We will notify affected parties of personal-data breaches in accordance with applicable laws.

10.Cookies & tracking

Our website uses a minimal set of first-party cookies and similar technologies to operate core features. We do not deploy third-party advertising trackers or behavioural-profiling cookies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. TBD by counsel — categorised cookie inventory will be appended.

11.Children’s privacy

Our services are intended for business use and are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children. If we become aware that we have collected such data without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to delete it.

12.Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. Material changes will be communicated through our website or by direct notice where appropriate.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this document indicates when the policy was most recently revised. We encourage you to review the policy periodically.
