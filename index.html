How it actually works

We close the loop on every customer interaction your business runs. From policy and delivery through governance and audit, then straight back into the next cycle.

  • Model A

    The deep partnership. We run the full lifecycle alongside your team, from policy intake through the auditor's signature.

  • Model B

    The plug-in path. You keep the systems you already trust, and we add only the AI and governance layers you need.

Either way, your data stays where it is, your governance stays in your hands, and what you learn this cycle carries straight into the next.

Model A. Full Lifecycle Partnership

  1. Design and Authorize

    Before any traffic flows, we sit down with your team and learn how your compliance world actually works. Your regulators. Your data residency rules. Your internal sign-off chain. Nothing goes live until your name is on the paperwork.

    Signed before traffic flows

  2. Build, Test, Migrate

    We work in two-week sprints with a green test suite at every demo, so you always know exactly where we are. When it's time to migrate, the new system runs alongside your legacy stack and we shift traffic over gradually. Nobody loses a message during cutover.

    No message lost in cutover

  3. Run at Scale

    Once you're live, your messages move on tier-1 carrier routes in under ten seconds, and we automatically reroute the moment a channel hiccups. Anything outside the policy you defined gets flagged with full context. Your team makes the call. We hold the conversation steady until you do.

    Your team always makes the call

  4. Audit and Renew

    At the close of every cycle, your compliance lead receives a single tamper-evident package. Every action, every approval, every signature, in whatever format your auditor wants. They co-sign, and what you learned this cycle goes straight into how we run the next one.

    Auditor signs once, learnings carry forward
Model B. Operate and Optimize

  1. Plug In, Don't Rip Out

    You've spent years building the stack you trust, and the last thing you need is another vendor asking you to rip it all out. Our plugins sit on top of what you already run. Your CRM. Your contact centre. Your messaging gateway. We're usually live the same day we sign. No migration, no retraining, no disruption to the team you've built.

    Live the same day, on the systems you already trust

  2. Govern the AI

    We know what stops AI projects in their tracks. The security review. So we don't ask your team to take a leap of faith. Every plugin runs inside the policy you wrote, with deterministic guardrails that decide what the model can and can't do before it ever speaks to a customer. Your CISO gets the audit trail. Your operations lead gets the speed. Nobody gets a 2 a.m. surprise.

    Speed your ops team wants, controls your CISO can defend

  3. Own the Loop

    The honest worry every buyer has is this: what happens to my data and my history if this vendor disappears? So we built it the other way around. Your conversations, your logs, your audit trail all stay in your environment. You can see every efficiency gain in real time, you can export everything in one click, and the kill switch is yours. If we ever stop earning the seat at your table, you walk away with the whole loop intact.

    Your data, your logs, your kill switch. Always yours.
