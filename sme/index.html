How it works

Three steps. Live before lunch.

Most SME onboardings finish in under 10 minutes. The slowest step is waiting for the SMS verification code — usually 30 seconds, sometimes 90 if your carrier is slow.

  1. 01

    Connect Lark.

    Open the DOLPHLINK app inside Lark App Center, click Authorise. We never read your existing Lark messages — we only post into a new group chat we create on first run.

    ~ 60 seconds

  2. 02

    Verify your business number.

    Enter the mobile number customers should see as the sender, click Verify. We send a one-time SMS code, you reply 6 digits in Lark. Done.

    ~ 90 seconds

  3. 03

    Send your first message.

    Pick a template (we ship 20+ pre-localised for common SME flows), or type your own. Send to one number, see the Lark thread populate with delivery + read receipts.

    ~ 5 minutes
