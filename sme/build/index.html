How the pieces fit together — three workflow shapes that cover most SMEs.

Whichever path you picked above, your real-world workflows usually fall into one of these three shapes. Mix and match.

Flow 01 · most common

ERP signal → team workspace alert

  1. Your ERPSAP / 用友 / 金蝶 / Odoo
  2. DOLPHLINKWebhook + routing
  3. WorkspaceLark / Slack / Teams
  4. Your teamSales / CS / warehouse

New order, low-stock alert, refund request — your ERP fires a webhook to us, we route it through the right template and post to the right team channel. Each message carries an "open in ERP" deep link.

Flow 02 · approval-driven

Approval flow → customer notification

  1. Customer askWhatsApp / form / SMS
  2. Approval flowMulti-level sign-off
  3. DOLPHLINKMulti-channel send
  4. CustomerSMS / WhatsApp / Email

Customer submits a quote, refund or address-change request. Your existing approval flow handles internal sign-off (with the audit trail your auditor wants). On approval, we reach the customer on their preferred channel.

Flow 03 · time-driven

Calendar event → automatic customer reminder

  1. CalendarEvent + customer phone
  2. DOLPHLINKCron + template render
  3. CustomerSMS / WhatsApp
  4. Workspace threadReply + status

Bookings, renewal calls, on-site service, contract expiry — a calendar event fires reminders 24h / 2h / 30min before. Multi-language, channel-routed, and customer replies land in the same workspace thread.

Your real stack is some mix of these. We'll figure out which mix in the call — usually 30 minutes is enough.
